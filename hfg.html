Heart Family Group

Heart Family Group was set up by Chanel’s mum, Fay, in 2014 with an aim to connect parents and patients affected by Congenital Heart Defects to support each other. Shortly joined by fellow heart parents Chanel Crane, Laura Lane and Gemma Kier.

Fay- ‘When Chanel was born we made the most amazing friendships with other families, it was the support from people who understood I needed. When we got home for the first time at 3 month old, I missed the support and them friendship’s’ - and so the Heart Family Group was born. Wanting all families to have support from others who can truly understand and in similar situations.

Heart Family Group has a private support group on Facebook, connecting families on social media. When possible we also try to organise annual meets ups too, usually at Alton Towers. This is always a magical experience for families to meet up with others and can be beneficial for the children aswell as the parents, most importantly meeting outside of hospital to just have fun with others that understand.

If you are affected by a Congenital Heart Defect please head towards our Facebook main page, Heart Family Group and private support group, Heart Family Group Members to find out more.

This video was created during Covid-19 to give our shielding families a feeling of togetherness when we were all kept apart.
